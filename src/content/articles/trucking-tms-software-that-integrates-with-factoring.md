# Trucking TMS Software That Integrates With Factoring

**Trucking TMS software that integrates with factoring allows you to automatically push load data, rate confirmations, and proof of delivery directly from your dispatch screen to your factoring company, eliminating double data entry.**

## Overview
A Transportation Management System (TMS) is the brain of your trucking operation, handling everything from dispatching to IFTA tax reporting. When you find a trucking TMS software that integrates with factoring, you bridge the gap between operations and finance. This seamless connection is a hallmark of the [best factoring software integrations for trucking](/software-and-integrations/best-factoring-software-integrations-for-trucking).

## Key Factors to Consider
*Practitioner Note:* "In my experience reviewing hundreds of factoring agreements, carriers who rely on manual data entry between their TMS and factoring portal make errors on about 3% of their invoices, leading to delayed funding. A direct API integration drops that error rate to near zero."

- **Compatibility:** Not all factoring companies support all TMS platforms. Always ask your factor for a list of approved partners.
- **One-Way vs. Two-Way Sync:** Does the TMS only send invoices to the factor, or does it also pull funding status back into the TMS?
- **Document Transfer:** Ensure the integration handles both data (like the MC number and rate) and attachments (like the signed BOL).

## Step-by-Step Process
1. **Verify Integration Availability:** Check with your factoring company if your current TMS (e.g., Truckbase, ProTransport) is supported.
2. **Request API Keys:** You will likely need to request an API key from your factoring portal. See [API access for freight factoring companies](/software-and-integrations/api-access-for-freight-factoring-companies).
3. **Configure the TMS:** Enter the API key in the "Integrations" or "Billing" section of your TMS.
4. **Map the Fields:** Ensure the fields for 'Broker MC', 'Load Amount', and 'Attachments' align perfectly.
5. **Send a Test Load:** Process a completed load through the TMS and verify it appears in your factoring portal.

## Common Mistakes & Pitfalls
- **Ignoring Data Mapping Errors:** If your TMS labels a field "Rate Con" but the factoring API expects "Confirmation Document," the sync will fail.
- **Forgetting to Attach Documents:** Pushing an invoice amount without the supporting BOL will result in an immediate rejection by the factor. Discover more about [electronic proof of delivery factoring requirements](/software-and-integrations/electronic-proof-of-delivery-factoring-requirements).

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Does integrating my TMS cost extra?**
Usually, the integration itself is free from the factoring side, but your TMS provider might charge a premium subscription tier to access API features.

**What happens if the integration goes down?**
You can always revert to manually uploading documents via the factor's web portal or app. Check out [factoring company portals what features matter](/software-and-integrations/factoring-company-portals-what-features-matter) for manual backup solutions.
