# Custom Dashboard for Freight Factoring Data

**A custom dashboard for freight factoring data visualizes critical financial metrics such as real-time reserve balances, aging reports, total fees paid, and pending fundings on a single, customizable screen.**

## Overview
Spreadsheets are prone to errors and quickly become outdated. Building or utilizing a custom dashboard for freight factoring data allows trucking company owners to check the financial health of their business at a glance. Advanced reporting is one of the features that separate basic companies from those offering the [best factoring software integrations for trucking](/software-and-integrations/best-factoring-software-integrations-for-trucking).

## Key Factors to Consider
- **The "At-Risk" Widget:** You need a visual warning (like a red chart) showing invoices that are approaching the 90-day recourse limit.
- **Data Exportability:** The dashboard must allow you to export data cleanly to CSV or Excel for your CPA.
- **API Availability:** To build a truly custom dashboard outside the factor's portal (e.g., using PowerBI or Tableau), you need [API access for freight factoring companies](/software-and-integrations/api-access-for-freight-factoring-companies).

## Step-by-Step Process
1. **Access Reporting Tools:** Log into your factoring portal and navigate to the "Reports" or "Dashboard" tab.
2. **Select Key Metrics:** Choose the widgets that matter most: Daily Funding, Reserve Balance, and Aging Summary.
3. **Set Up Alerts:** Configure the dashboard to highlight anomalies, such as an invoice that has been pending approval for more than 48 hours.
4. **Schedule Automated Reports:** Set the dashboard to automatically email a weekly summary to you and your accountant.

## Common Mistakes & Pitfalls
- **Data Overload:** Putting too many charts on the screen makes it unreadable. Focus only on cash flow and risk (aging).
- **Ignoring the Data:** A dashboard is useless if you don't check it daily. Make it the homepage on your office browser. Read [factoring company portals what features matter](/software-and-integrations/factoring-company-portals-what-features-matter).

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
**Can I integrate my TMS data into my factoring dashboard?**
Usually, the data flows the other way—factoring data is pulled into your TMS dashboard. See [trucking TMS software that integrates with factoring](/software-and-integrations/trucking-tms-software-that-integrates-with-factoring).
**Are custom dashboards expensive?**
Most modern factoring companies include dashboard tools for free, but building your own custom external dashboard via API will require developer costs.
