# How Long Does It Take to Process Factoring Invoices Online

**When submitted through a modern portal before the daily cutoff time, it typically takes 1 to 4 hours to process factoring invoices online, with same-day wire transfers available if approved.**

## Overview
Speed is the primary reason trucking companies use factoring. Wondering how long does it take to process factoring invoices online is the most common question among new owner-operators. If you are using the [best factoring software integrations for trucking](/software-and-integrations/best-factoring-software-integrations-for-trucking), the delay is almost entirely eliminated by automation.

## Key Factors to Consider
- **The Cutoff Time:** Most factoring companies have a hard cutoff (e.g., 11:00 AM EST) for same-day wires. If you submit at 11:05 AM, you won't see the money until tomorrow.
- **First-Time Brokers:** If this is the first time you are hauling for a specific broker, the factor must perform a manual credit check and Notice of Assignment setup, which can add 24 to 48 hours to the processing time.
- **Submission Quality:** A blurry BOL will trigger a manual review, halting the automated processing pipeline. See [electronic proof of delivery factoring requirements](/software-and-integrations/electronic-proof-of-delivery-factoring-requirements).

## Step-by-Step Process
1. **Prepare the Documents:** Ensure your Rate Confirmation and BOL are saved as clear, legible PDFs.
2. **Submit via Portal:** Upload the documents to your factoring software or use an integration like [trucking TMS software that integrates with factoring](/software-and-integrations/trucking-tms-software-that-integrates-with-factoring).
3. **Automated Verification:** The software instantly checks the broker's MC number against your credit limit.
4. **Manual Review (If Needed):** A human agent verifies the signatures on the BOL.
5. **Funding Release:** Once approved, the funds are batched and sent via ACH (next day) or Wire Transfer (same day).

## Common Mistakes & Pitfalls
- **Missing Cutoff Times Due to Time Zones:** Submitting at 11:00 AM PST when your factor's cutoff is 11:00 AM EST means you missed it by three hours.
- **Incomplete Paperwork:** Forgetting to include a lumper receipt will cause the invoice to sit in "Pending Status" indefinitely. Read [common errors submitting factoring invoices electronically](/software-and-integrations/common-errors-submitting-factoring-invoices-electronically).

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)
**Is ACH faster than a Wire Transfer?**
No. ACH transfers usually clear the next business morning. Wire transfers process within hours on the same day but often come with a $15-$30 fee.
**Why is my invoice stuck in processing?**
Usually, it means the factoring company is trying to contact the broker to verify the load was actually delivered without claims.
